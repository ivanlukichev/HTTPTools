Line Sorter

Sort text lines alphabetically, reverse their order or remove duplicates directly in your browser. Useful for keyword lists, notes, exports and text cleanup.

Sort mode

Paste line-based text, choose your options and process it locally in your browser.

How it works

This tool sorts text line by line and can also remove duplicates, trim extra spaces and clean empty lines. It is useful for working with keyword lists, exports, tags, notes and generated content.

All processing happens locally in your browser. No text is uploaded or stored.

Examples

Example input:
banana
apple
apple
 cherry

Output with A-Z + remove duplicates + trim spaces:
apple
banana
cherry

FAQ

Can I sort lines alphabetically?

Yes. Choose Sort A-Z or Sort Z-A and process your line-based text.

Can I remove duplicates?

Yes. Enable Remove duplicates before processing.

Does this tool upload my text?

No. Everything stays in your browser.
