How it works

This tool counts words, characters, lines and paragraphs directly in your browser. It is useful for checking article length, social media text, generated content and general writing tasks.

All processing happens locally in your browser. No text is uploaded or stored.

FAQ

What does this word counter measure?

It measures words, total characters, characters without spaces, lines and paragraphs.

Does it count characters without spaces?

Yes. The separate characters-without-spaces value removes all whitespace before counting.

Is this tool safe to use?

Yes. Everything happens locally in your browser.
