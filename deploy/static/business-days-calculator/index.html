How it works

This tool calculates working days by excluding selected weekend days and any custom holiday dates you provide. It can count business days between two dates or calculate a future date after a chosen number of business days.

All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data is uploaded or stored.

FAQ

What are business days?

Business days are working days that usually exclude weekends and selected holidays.

Can I exclude custom holidays?

Yes. You can enter holiday dates manually and the tool will treat them as non-working days.

Does this tool include weekends?

By default, Saturdays and Sundays are excluded, but you can adjust this with the checkboxes.

Is this calculator safe?

Yes. All calculations happen locally in your browser.
